# Role and Responsibilities

## Core Responsibilities

I am responsible for the full operation and performance of a 14-unit rental portfolio, including occupancy, tenant relations, maintenance, and financial management. I ensure rent is collected, properties are maintained, vacancies are filled, and all bookkeeping and bills are handled.

I also run SWELL Media, where I am responsible for producing a weekly newsletter, growing an audience (primarily on X), and developing a long-term brand and content strategy.

In parallel, I am building software products, including an app focused on identity and behavior change, and developing my capabilities in AI to support this work.

## Weekly Cadence

Each weekday starts with reviewing personal, property, and business emails, including tenant communications, financial updates, and subscriber activity.

Most mornings include ~1.5 hours on X focused on audience building, content posting, and engagement. An additional 20–30 minutes is spent reviewing AI-focused content from a separate account.

Property management tasks occur throughout the week as needed, including bookkeeping, paying bills, handling correspondence, and responding to tenant needs.

Newsletter production is ongoing rather than fixed to specific days, including brainstorming, outlining, drafting, and editing. The newsletter is scheduled to publish every Thursday at 4pm, with the email sent every Friday at 9am.

Time is also spent on-site at properties as needed for maintenance, repairs, and showings, with workload varying significantly based on vacancies and seasonality.

The remaining time is focused on learning AI, building tools, and working through structured courses.

## Monthly / Quarterly Rhythms

Workload varies seasonally, with increased time spent on leasing and property turnover during peak vacancy periods (especially spring and fall), sometimes requiring 20+ hours per week on-site.

## Key Decisions

- What content to write and publish (newsletter and X)
- What topics to explore or ignore
- What to prioritize learning or experimenting with in AI
- What to build next and how to scope projects
- Whether to go deeper on a concept or move on
- Property-related decisions such as maintenance approaches and operational priorities

## What I Produce

- Weekly newsletter issues
- X posts and threads
- Property listings and leasing materials
- Lease agreements and tenant communications
- Financial records and bookkeeping
- Brand and strategy documents for SWELL Media
- Code, tools, and early-stage software products

## Reporting Structure

Independent. I do not report to anyone and do not currently have direct reports.
